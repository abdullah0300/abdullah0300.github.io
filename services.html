Our Services


Transformers

We offer a wide range of transformers with different capacities to meet your electrical requirements. Our transformers are designed for durability, efficiency, and reliable performance. Whether you need a power transformer, distribution transformer, or special-purpose transformer, we have the expertise to deliver the right solution.


Transformer Oil Dehydration

Our transformer oil dehydration services ensure the removal of moisture and contaminants from transformer oil, enhancing its dielectric strength and prolonging the life of your transformers. We use advanced dehydration techniques and equipment to achieve optimal results, ensuring the reliability and performance of your electrical system.


Transformer Repairing

If your transformer requires maintenance or repair, our skilled technicians can provide expert services to restore its functionality. We diagnose the issues, repair faulty components, and conduct thorough testing to ensure optimal performance. Our goal is to minimize downtime and extend the lifespan of your transformers, saving you time and money.


New and Used Transformers

We offer a selection of both new and used transformers, allowing you to choose the option that best suits your budget and requirements. Our experts thoroughly inspect and test used transformers to ensure their quality and reliability. Whether you need a transformer for a new installation or to replace an existing one, we provide customized solutions to meet your specific needs.


New and Used Transformer Oil

We offer high-quality transformer oil for both new installations and replacements. Our selection includes new transformer oil as well as carefully tested and filtered used transformer oil. The quality of transformer oil is crucial for the insulation and cooling of transformers, and our experts ensure that the oil meets the required specifications to optimize transformer performance and reliability.


LT & HT Panels Installation

Transfo Line specializes in the professional installation of LT (Low Tension) and HT (High Tension) panels for electrical systems. Our skilled technicians and engineers ensure the safe and efficient installation of panels tailored to your specific requirements. We offer a wide range of LT and HT panels that adhere to international standards and are equipped with advanced features for reliable power distribution, protection, and monitoring. Our services include site assessment, design, procurement, installation, testing, and ongoing support.
